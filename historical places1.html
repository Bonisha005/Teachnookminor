Historical Places in India

Taj Mahal, Agra

The Taj Mahal, built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan, is an epitome of love and a UNESCO World Heritage site.

Qutub Minar, Delhi

The Qutub Minar is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and the tallest brick minaret in the world.

Hawa Mahal, Jaipur

The Hawa Mahal, or "Palace of Winds," is an architectural marvel in Jaipur built for royal women to observe street festivities.

Mysore Palace, Karnataka

The Mysore Palace is a historical and architectural landmark, known for its intricate design and grand illumination during the Dussehra festival.

Gateway of India, Mumbai

The Gateway of India is an iconic monument built during the 20th century in Mumbai, serving as a major tourist attraction and a historical landmark.

Charminar, Hyderabad

Charminar, located in Hyderabad, India, is a historic monument known for its stunning Indo-Islamic architecture and four grand arches.
